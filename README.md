📌 Project Title

Healthy Roots, Stronger Future

📖 Overview

This project was an 8-week community engagement initiative conducted at Ashiana-e-Korang Orphanage, Islamabad. The goal was to contribute to the emotional, educational, and social development of children aged 5–15.

🎯 Objectives
Improve confidence and self-expression in children
Provide academic support
Encourage physical activity and teamwork
Promote moral and ethical values

🛠 Activities Conducted
Academic tutoring sessions
Sports and physical activities
Creative arts and drawing
Roleplays and storytelling
Movie-based learning sessions

🌍 SDGs Addressed
SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being
SDG 4: Quality Education
SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities
SDG 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions

📈 Impact
Improved confidence and communication skills among children
Better academic understanding
Increased teamwork and social interaction
Personal growth and leadership development for volunteers
